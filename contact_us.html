About Our Project

SynthCV is a smart and free resume maker website that helps users create professional resumes easily. It not only designs attractive,ATS-friendly resumes but also builds an online profile where employers can visit and hire you directly. SynthCV uses AI to automatically update your resume according to job descriptions, ensuring it always matches the position you apply for. The platform is linked with LinkedIn, allowing you to import your details instantly and keep your resume up to date. Users can track profile visits, get AI-based suggestions, and create multiple resumes for different roles. SynthCV is more than just a resume builder — it’s a complete career assistant that connects job seekers with recruiters, saves time, and helps you stand out in the competitive job market.
